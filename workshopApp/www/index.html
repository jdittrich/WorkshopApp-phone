This app guides youthrough a Design studio Workshop, an idea-generation method

Gather 2-5 People to do the workshop.

It needs to be clear what should archived,so you need a problem to solve or a challange

Please take care that you have the following items:

  • A bunch of paper sheets (DINA4 DINA3) for each person
  • Something to draw for everybody (felt pens are great)
  • several green and red crayons or felt pens
begin workshop!
1design

In the design phase, everybody starts with an empty sheet of paper. Everyone draws his/her solutions.

Each paper must be devided in 6 or 4 sections to host different solutions each

You got 5 min. in which you all work in paralell on your own ideas.

Show Example Present and Review
1presentRev

For each person:

  1. Briefly present your Solution
  2. Than, everybody else gives Feedback.

5 min per person for presentation and feedback

This is not a design phase! Discuss what is good or bad about ideas and why, but leave the design up to the creator of the sketches.

Everybody presented and got feedback?

After each person presented his/her work and got feedback from the group, start another round of design. Please note that it is o.k. to make use of the other's ideas!
